QuScope — Quantum Algorithms for Electron Microscopy

An open-source Python package implementing quantum circuit simulations of CTEM, STEM, EELS, SAED, CBED, Kikuchi patterns, and ptychography. Validated against classical methods on MoS₂ and executed on IBM quantum hardware. Developed through Northwestern's MICRO Program under Dr. Roberto dos Reis.

Heteroatom-Doped Graphene Quantum Dots for ORR Catalysis

High-throughput DFT screening of N, B, S, F, P single-doped and co-doped graphene quantum dots as electrocatalysts for the oxygen reduction reaction in AEMFCs. Automated pipeline generating a combinatorial library across six GQD sizes with CHE thermodynamic analysis. Targeting ACS Applied Nano Materials or JPCC.
